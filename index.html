About Us

We source premium wild truffles directly from the heart of the Carpathian forests in Romania. Carefully handpicked and selected by expert foragers, our truffles are delivered fresh and fast to meet the highest culinary standards. We operate seasonally and deliver up to 100 kg per week to restaurants, distributors, and culinary professionals across Europe and the UK.
